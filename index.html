About Us

SERVING SINCE 2019

We are based in Faridpur but we want to establish a sustainable self-dependency through the online employment model all over the 64 districts of Bangladesh.

Innovative IT Firm was founded by two professionals, young, creative and motivated persons with a vision to take FREELANCING as a career and train people to reduce the unemployment problem in Bangladesh. There are not enough government or non-government jobs available. As a result, Over 4.2% of people in Bangladesh are unemployed. So, we think that FREELANCING can be the best career for them. There are many opportunities over there, one has to take that opportunity by taking any related training and jump into it. And so we aim to create professionals for the national and international marketplaces by providing world-class smart IT professionals to represent Bangladesh in the world.

LET US GO FORWARD IN THIS BATTLE FORTIFIED BY CONVICTION THAT THOSE WHO LABOUR IN THE SERVICE OF A GREAT AND GOOD CAUSE WILL NEVER FAIL.
